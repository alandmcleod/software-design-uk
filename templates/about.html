About

Clear thinking for software, systems, and delivery decisions

Software Design UK is a professional presence built around practical architecture guidance, business analysis, cloud delivery thinking, and modern AI-assisted ways of working.

The aim is simple: help teams explain complex technical choices in language that is useful to stakeholders, delivery teams, and decision-makers.

The site focuses on grounded ideas rather than hype: trade-offs, documentation quality, integration patterns, and how AI can support analysis without replacing judgement.
