## Useful starting points

Drafting stakeholder questions, decomposing large requests, and summarising meeting notes are all good tasks for assisted analysis.

## Where caution matters

Generated output still needs challenge and validation. Analysts should treat AI as a collaborator for exploration, not as an authority.

## A practical mindset

The real opportunity is not replacing workshops or critical thinking. It is reducing blank-page effort so more time goes into sharper conversations.
